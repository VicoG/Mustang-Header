About This Site

Cars and Travel

I worked for Lawrence Livermore Labs where I designed and developed many sites. Unfortunately, all of my work is behind a firewall so no one can see what I have done. That does not make for a very interesting portfolio page. So to showcase my abilities I created mockups of work I did so during interviews there is a visual representation of my work. But I also want to showcase my design abilities.

That is where this site comes into play. When thinking about building a site to demonstrate my abilities, I needed inspiration. So I looked at what I enjoy. That's traveling (adventure really) and cars, preferably fast cars.

So the theme of this site is my beloved and departed 2008 Mustang and some road trips we shared together.

But because I also need to showcase my skills, this site incorporates use of Photoshop to enhance my photos, and Javascript to make it all just a bit more interesting. I hope you enjoy.
